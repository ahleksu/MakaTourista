About Us

MakaTourista, the Philippines' largest travel platform, helps 150,000 travellers each month make every trip their best one. Travellers across the globe and country will be able to experience Makati City as they experience the city's wonderful restaurants, hotels, museums, tours and many more.

Our Mission

As one of the country's largest travel platform, MakaTourista enables Makati City, and any other city in the country, to reach an audience around the world, showcasing what it's like to be in the Philippines while assuring that it is safe to travel in its wonderful cityscapes.

History

Founded in 2020 in Makati City, MakaTourista has grown from a simple travel blog into one of the country's leading travel websites. MakaTourista's mission is to showcase Makati City's best destinations while providing safe destinations for the new normal. As the pandemic wears down, MakaTourista will continue to lead and revive people's spirit for travelling.
