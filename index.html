Welcome to Makati City

Makati is one of the most urban cities in the Philippines, famous for its skyscrapers, upscale shopping malls, restaurants, and entertainment hubs. This city is known as a business district and the financial hub of the country. However, thanks to its community of diverse cultures, nationalities, and socio-economic backgrounds, it is a must-see destination for both local and international travellers.
